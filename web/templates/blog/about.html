Abisoye Adekoya

Abisoye has been in the financial services industry since 1980. He holds both a Series 7 and Series 24 License. He is a 1977 graduate of the University of Illinois at Chicago with a Bachelors degree in Marketing. In 2005 he received an LLM in Taxation from the St. Thomas School of Law.

Abisoye has been an Enrolled Agent, as designated by the Department of the Treasury since 1990. He passed all four parts of the Special Enrollment Examination on his first attempt. Abisoye is a member of the National Association of Enrolled Agents.

Over the years his practice has grown at a steady rate, with increasing focus on small and medium size enterprises. The retention of clients year after year has been outstanding. Some new clients have come from the large chain tax preparation companies. Many new clients say that his service is excellent and his pricing is very competitive.

Abisoye is a long-time resident of Chicago where he enjoys playing golf, racquetball, skiing and traveling.
